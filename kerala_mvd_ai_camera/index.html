About Kerala MVD AI Cameras

The Kerala MVD AI cameras are a cornerstone of the Safe Kerala Project, an initiative by the Kerala Motor Vehicles Department (MVD) to enhance road safety and enforce traffic regulations. Launched to curb rising road accidents and violations, these advanced cameras leverage artificial intelligence to monitor highways and major roads across Kerala. They are strategically placed in high-traffic areas, such as Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, and Kozhikode, to ensure comprehensive surveillance.

Equipped with cutting-edge technology, MVD AI cameras detect a range of violations, including overspeeding, not wearing seat belts or helmets, using mobile phones while driving, and signal jumping. The cameras capture high-resolution images and videos, which are processed in real-time to issue automated challans (fines) to violators. This system reduces human intervention, ensuring transparency and efficiency in enforcement. According to MVD reports, the deployment of AI cameras has led to a significant reduction in traffic violations and accidents since their introduction.

Our interactive map provides real-time access to the locations of these cameras, helping drivers stay informed and avoid unintentional violations. By promoting awareness, Kerala MVD AI Camera Tracker aims to foster safer driving habits and support the MVD’s mission of creating accident-free roads. Whether you’re a daily commuter or a traveler, use our map to navigate Kerala’s roads confidently and responsibly.

Driving Tips to Avoid Violations

To ensure safe driving and avoid fines from Kerala MVD AI cameras, follow these practical tips:

  • Adhere to Speed Limits: Always follow posted speed limits, especially on highways where AI cameras are prevalent.
  • Wear Safety Gear: Drivers and passengers must wear seat belts, and two-wheeler riders should wear helmets at all times.
  • Avoid Distractions: Refrain from using mobile phones while driving to prevent penalties and ensure focus.
  • Follow Traffic Signals: Obey traffic lights and road signs to avoid signal-jumping violations.
  • Check Vehicle Compliance: Ensure your vehicle has valid documents (e.g., registration, insurance) to avoid additional fines.

By adopting these habits, you contribute to safer roads and minimize the risk of penalties from MVD AI cameras.
